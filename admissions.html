So, how can YOU enroll?

Our Process, It's pretty simple, really!

If you are a top performer in school and every passionate about acheiving high-we are here to help you.Our
admission process will make sure that it is a WIN-WIN for both students and the organization

Initial Application

application link shared soon after higher secondary exam results are published.You will need to provide your 10th and 12th marks for initial screening

Eligibility Interview

Our hope3 volunteers will screen each selected students to assess their financial need and passion for learning

Document Verification

All your documents will be verified for authenticity our Admission team

Our team educational professionals will interview the students or admission and also offer guidance on college selection
