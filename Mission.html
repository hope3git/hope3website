our mission & vision

HOPE 3 Mission

Our mission is to:
  1. Identify and provided financial assistance for higher education for circumstantially challenged, earnest students
  2. Form long term partnerships with educators, subject matter experts, technology and media industries.
  3. Connect students with our educational and industry partners through:
    1. An online mentored, collaborative, immersive learning platform.
    2. Projects of relevance to industry and research.
    3. Internship opportunities, Part-time and Full-time Job opportunities with our industry partners.
    4. Guidance and support for entrepreneurial ventures.

HOPE 3 Vision

Empowering circumstantially challenged,aspiring talent to realize their true potential and become next generation leaders.
