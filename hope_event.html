Paramapatham: Game Analysis & Variations

Game dynamics through computer simulation

Ganesh used python to simulate and understand ...Paramapatham a.k.a Snake & Ladders, a common Indian game. Here he computed the number of steps needed to complete the game by a single player by taking: just the basic board without a snake or a ladder, then he added a single snake, a single ladder, the total game and looked at how the distribution changed. Further he experimented with creative variations of the game by breaking the linearity and of the game by creating a die that can throw negative numbers and other variations. Overall he demonstrated the incredible creativity and analysis that is possible in game design using computer simulations.
