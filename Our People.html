our people

There is an army of people making this HOPE3 a reality. We have industrial partners where our selected students are given the opportunity to have real world experience while they are still studying in college. Our Board members are the pillars of the organization taking care of all day to day activities from admissions, student services, crafting Varsity courses, guiding volunteers and many more. Finance team meticulously tracks all income and expenses and makes sure the organization is in compliance with the laws. Admissions team takes care of the entire interview process starting from initial screening to final Varsity onboarding. HOPE3 media team assists students to bring out their talents via entertaining online content and help with other design projects. All of this is made possible by the financial support provided by our Donors. Our Alumni offers a great mentorship program to all current students guiding them based on their valuable experience.
